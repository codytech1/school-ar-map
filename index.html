How to Use

This section introduces you to the School AR Map experience. It gives a quick overview of how to use the webapp From navigation highlights to basic interaction steps, this guide helps you get oriented with the AR environment.

  1. Open the website on your phone or tablet.
  2. Download and install the APK file
  3. Open the unity app on on your android device
  4. Point your camera at the designated AR marker.
  5. Watch as detailed information appears in AR!
  6. click here to learn more.
